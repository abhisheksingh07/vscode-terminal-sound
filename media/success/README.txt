DROP YOUR CUSTOM SUCCESS SOUNDS HERE
======================================
Place any .mp3 or .wav files in this folder.
They will automatically appear in the sound selector.

To select a sound:
  Open Command Palette (Ctrl+Shift+P) → "PlaySound: Select Success Sound"

Built-in sounds:
  success-1.mp3 - High ding (1000Hz)
  success-2.mp3 - Chime (880Hz)
  success-3.mp3 - Quick pop (1200Hz)
